Welcome!

Note: Charts are best viewed on a desktop. Graphs might not work on mobile devices.

This dynamic app keeps track of a business' incomes, expense and profit.

To enter new data, click on "Add new transaction" on the top-left corner of the screen and fill out the form.

The new data entries will be displayed in the 'Transaction History' section and the chart will be updated.

This app has been filled with random data to show what this app is capable of.

You can also clear the sample data and entire your own data.
